What Is Junk Removal?
Junk removal is a labor-inclusive pickup service. A crew comes to your location, loads everything, and hauls it away. You don't rent a dumpster. You don't lift anything. Items go to donation centers, recycling facilities, or transfer stations depending on type and condition.
It's different from curbside pickup — crews take items from inside your home, garage, basement, or yard.

Electronics & Recycling in Oregon
Oregon has specific rules around e-waste. Most electronics cannot go to landfill under the Oregon E-Cycles program. This includes:
Televisions (CRT, LCD, plasma)
Desktop and laptop computers
Computer monitors
Tablets and e-readers (varies by location)
Where electronics go locally: Washington County operates drop-off sites for e-waste. Many junk removal services in the Beaverton area sort electronics separately and route them to certified recyclers rather than the transfer station.
If you're handing off electronics as part of a larger haul, ask specifically how they're handled.
Does junk removal in Beaverton OR include same-day service?
Some crews offer same-day availability, but it depends on scheduling. It's worth calling in the morning to check. Weekday mornings tend to have more flexibility than weekend slots.
What's the difference between junk removal and a dumpster rental?
With junk removal, a crew loads everything for you. With a dumpster rental, you load it yourself over a set number of days. For large cleanouts where you need time, a dumpster may be cheaper. For single-day jobs or items you can't move yourself, junk removal is usually faster and more practical.
Are there items junk removal crews won't take in Beaverton?
Yes. Hazardous waste — paint, chemicals, motor oil, propane, asbestos — typically cannot be hauled. These require special disposal through Washington County's hazardous waste program. Most other household and commercial items are fine.
Do I need to be home during pickup?
Not always. Some services allow a designated contact or photos in advance to confirm scope. Confirm with your specific crew when booking.
