## What Is Junk Removal?

Junk removal is a labor-inclusive pickup service. A crew comes to your location, loads everything, and hauls it away. No dumpster rental. No heavy lifting on your end.

Items go to donation centers, recycling facilities, or transfer stations depending on type and condition. It's different from curbside pickup — crews take items from inside your home, garage, basement, or yard.

## Services Available in Beaverton OR

Most junk removal services in this area handle:

- **Furniture** — sofas, mattresses, bed frames, dressers, recliners
- **Appliances** — washers, dryers, refrigerators, dishwashers
- **Electronics** — TVs, monitors, computers, printers *(see e-waste section)*
- **Yard debris** — branches, soil, sod, fencing, outdoor furniture
- **Construction debris** — drywall scraps, lumber, flooring materials
- **Estate and garage cleanouts** — full-property haul-aways
- **Metal and scrap** — old tools, pipes, structural metal
- **Office equipment** — desks, chairs, filing cabinets, breakroom appliances

> **Not typically accepted:** Hazardous materials, paint, chemicals, propane tanks, asbestos-containing materials.

## Pricing Reference

Pricing follows a load-based model. Volume, item type, labor required, and accessibility all affect the final number.

| Load Size | Description | Typical Range |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| Single item | Sofa, mattress, or appliance | From $99 |
| Small load | ¼ truck or less | $150 – $250 |
| Medium load | ½ truck | $350 – $500 |
| Large cleanout | Full truck | $550 – $900+ |

**Factors that affect price:**

- Floor level and staircase access
- Item disassembly required
- Distance from door to truck
- Volume of load
- Item type (metal vs. upholstered vs. hazardous-adjacent)

A refrigerator on the ground floor costs less to remove than one in a basement with a narrow staircase. For a full breakdown of what's included in a standard haul, see the [local hauling service details](https://junkremovalbeavertonor.com/services/).

## How the Process Works

1. **Request a quote** — describe what you have and roughly how much; online or by phone
2. **Schedule pickup** — most services offer next-day or same-week windows; same-day depends on crew availability
3. **Crew arrives** — they assess the load on-site and confirm the final price before starting
4. **Loading** — the crew handles all carrying, lifting, and loading
5. **Haul-away** — items go to the appropriate facility (transfer station, donation drop, recycler)
6. **Done** — no cleanup required on your end

## Electronics & Recycling in Oregon

Oregon has specific rules around e-waste. Most electronics **cannot go to landfill** under the **Oregon E-Cycles program**.

### Covered Under Oregon E-Cycles

- Televisions (CRT, LCD, plasma)
- Desktop and laptop computers
- Computer monitors
- Tablets and e-readers *(varies by drop-off location)*

### Where Electronics Go Locally

Washington County operates drop-off sites for e-waste. Many junk removal services in the Beaverton area sort electronics separately and route them to certified recyclers rather than the transfer station.

> If you're handing off electronics as part of a larger haul, ask specifically how they're handled before booking.

## FAQ

### Does junk removal in Beaverton OR include same-day service?

Some crews offer same-day availability, but it depends on scheduling. Weekday mornings tend to have more open slots than weekends. Worth calling the morning of to check.

### What's the difference between junk removal and a dumpster rental?

With junk removal, a crew loads everything for you. With a dumpster rental, you load it yourself over a set number of days. For large cleanouts where you need time, a dumpster may be cheaper. For single-day jobs or items you can't move yourself, junk removal is usually faster.

### Are there items junk removal crews won't take in Beaverton?

Yes. Hazardous waste — paint, chemicals, motor oil, propane, asbestos — typically cannot be hauled. These require special disposal through Washington County's hazardous waste program. Most other household and commercial items are accepted.

### Do I need to be home during pickup?

Not always. Some services allow a designated contact or advance photos to confirm scope. Confirm with your specific crew when booking.

### How is pricing confirmed?

Most crews give a firm price on-site after seeing the load — before any work starts. You're not obligated to proceed if the number doesn't work for you.
